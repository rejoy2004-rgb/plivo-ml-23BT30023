Manifold Realism (PCA Subspace Search)

To restrict search coordinates to the manifold of realistic human voices, style tensors (raw dimensionality: 130,560) are projected onto a 15-dimensional subspace computed via Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) of the 54 stock voices. This captures 88% of vocal variance, preventing the optimization algorithm from drifting into adversarial regions of high similarity but degraded audio.

Evolutionary Search & Guards

  • Warm-Start Initialization: Initializes from the best convex combination of stock voices found via Dirichlet sampling on the weight simplex.
  • Separable Diagonal CMA-ES: Operates directly in the 15-dimensional PCA space, learning dimension-wise standard deviations to focus search steps.
  • Anti-Overfitting Loop: Rotates texts per generation to avoid prosody overfitting, and validates candidates against full reference sentences before updating the elite.
  • Plateau & Noise Scheduling: Scales down step size sigma automatically if no fitness improvement is detected for 8 consecutive generations.
